B.Pharm

B Pharmacy or B Pharma full form is Bachelor of Pharmacy, it is an undergraduate 4-year degree program in pharmacy. It provides an extensive education in pharmacy from pharmacology and pharmaceutical chemistry to the actual practice of and technology related to the Pharmacy Process.

The duration of the B Pharmacy course is four years. In most cases, you are required to pass a higher secondary education or 10+2 with subjects in the science stream such as Physics, Chemistry, and Biology for admission into any B Pharmacy program. B Pharmacy is a required degree for entering into the pharmaceutical industry, healthcare sector, or research and development.

To become a B Pharm professional, some of the common areas are the Pharmaceutical industry, the Healthcare sector, and Research & Development. It prepares students for working as pharmacists, researchers, or in many roles within the pharmaceutical and healthcare industries.
