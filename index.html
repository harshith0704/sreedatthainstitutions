An Autonomous Institution

The SDGI was established in 2010 by the Vyjayanthi Educational Society, which was founded in 2000 by Sri G. Panduranga Reddy. The institutions' vision is to prepare students for the changing workplace by providing technical knowledge, flexibility, and an understanding of the corporate world. The College is approved by the AICTE, New Delhi, and affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad. Since its inception in 2010, the institute has grown beyond leaps and bounds to become a premier institute in the state on par with several top institutes.
