Pharm.D

The Pharm D full form is a Doctorate of Pharmacy. The Pharm D duration is 6 years and is a professional doctoral program in Pharmacy designed to prepare students for clinical practice. Pharm D course covers subjects like pharmacology, clinical pharmacy, medicinal chemistry, and therapeutics, providing in-depth knowledge of medications and patient care. Pharm D course graduates play a crucial role in healthcare by collaborating with doctors to optimize treatment outcomes.

To be eligible for the Pharm D course, candidates must have passed Class 12 in the Science stream with Physics, Chemistry and Biology subjects with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. Pharm D course admission is majorly based on entrance scores, however, some colleges also accept Class 12 scores. The popular entrance exam scores that are accepted by colleges offering a Pharm D course are TS EAMCET, KCET, and NEET. The average Pharm D course fees for the Doctor of Pharmacy range between INR 2.3 Lakh and INR 9.6 Lakh.

Upon completion of the Pharm D course and passing licensure exams, pharmacists can work in a wide range of settings including community pharmacies, hospitals, clinics, pharmaceutical industry, academia, and government agencies. After completing the Pharm D course, graduates can choose from a variety of employment positions, including Clinical Pharmacists, Hospital Pharmacy Directors, Hospital Staff Pharmacists, Retail Pharmacists, Drug Experts, and Pharmacists. The average salary that a Pharm D course graduate earns ranges between INR 2.30 LPA and INR 10 LPA.
